What is a Game Developer:

The title of 'Game Developer' is a broad term, that includes: designers, story writters, coders, ect. Basically, programers and software developers turn the idea into code, while Designers and Artist produce the final product. At the entry level, game programmers usually use basic tools and languages. More importantly, they're expected to keep up with changing technology.
